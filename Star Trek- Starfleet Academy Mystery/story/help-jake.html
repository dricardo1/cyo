"Jake!"

You rush towards the thing. But when you hit it, it vanishes into a cloud of fabric. Suddenly, you feel a strong grip on your arm. It's Jake.

"Come on," you say."We've got to get out of here."

Jake says nothing. Something isn't right. You notice two small holes in his neck. Just then, you feel a pair of cold, strong arms close around you from behind. Cold fangs dig into your neck, and you fall dead to the floor, only to rise again, cursed, as a vampire.

Look at that... you've gone and gotten tangled with a vampire. That sucks!
