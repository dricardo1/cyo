You walk out the door to the house. The front gate is wide open, as if bidding you leave.

Looks like I'm the only one who made it out alive. I've got to leave this awful place and never come back.

"Let's get out of here," says Brad urgently. "We need to leave."

"Amen."

"What happened to Jake?"

"Some kind of creature got him."

"You mean he's dead?"

"Worse."

"Oh my God."

"I don't know. I was hoping he was with you."

"..."

You walk away from the house. A few minutes down the street, you decide to turn back. There is no house, only an empty, decaying lot where the house was. A cold gust of wind blows through dead branches overhead, and you shiver.

Never speak of this again, you think. Never speak of this again.

You got out alive, but the mystery of Bedford Manor remains forever unsolved.
