“I’m not Hungry, thanks.” “Oh, but I haven’t even told you what the main course is!” You back away. He approaches you slowly, brandishing the knife. *I can guess what he has in mind for dinner.* “Now stand still!” he screams, jumping on you. “And let me cut you a good piece!” You scream and kick as he hacks you to pieces with the old knife. And he laughs. And laughs, grins, laughs, laughs, laughs… What a delicious turn of events!
